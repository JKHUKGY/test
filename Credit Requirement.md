# Credit Requirement — ENGR 321 / 601 FAQ

---

### Does ENGR 321 / 601 maintain my full-time student status?

- **ENGR 601 (full-time internship):** Awards **10 CR**, which **does** maintain full-time status.
- **ENGR 321 / part-time ENGR 601:** Does **not** grant full-time status. Internships are presumed to occur during your vacation quarter.

---

### Do I qualify for ISHIP when doing an internship?

Yes. You can add ISHIP coverage from your **MyUW** page.  
For assistance or if you cannot add coverage yourself, contact: [engrint@uw.edu](mailto:engrint@uw.edu)

---

### Can I defer ENGR 321 / 601 (e.g., take it in Autumn for a Summer internship)?

**No.** UW policy (Registrar's Office, International Student Services) requires students to take internship credit **simultaneously** with the internship.  
For international students, USCIS policy similarly requires CPT credit to be taken at the same time as the internship.

---

### Are there other fees associated with ENGR 321 / 601?

**No.** Only the course fee listed in the course table should be assessed to your account.  
Fees such as **IMA** and **UPASS** should not be added during a full-time internship.

- If IMA or UPASS fees are erroneously added:
  - UW Transportation: [transportation.uw.edu](https://transportation.uw.edu)
  - IMA: [washington.edu/ima](https://washington.edu/ima)
- If tuition assessment is incorrect, email: [engrint@uw.edu](mailto:engrint@uw.edu)

---

### Is ENGR 320 going to be offered again?

**No.** ENGR 320 has been retired at the request of UW ISS and the UW Registrar's Office, for better compliance with UW and USCIS policies.
