# ENGR 321 Registration Form Guide

This document outlines all fields and requirements for the ENGR 321 internship credit registration form.

---

## Section 1 — Basic Information

### Email
- **Field:** Your UW email address
- **Required:** Yes

---

## Section 2 — Acknowledgements

You must acknowledge **all** of the following statements:

- [ ] I have reviewed the **REQUIRED ASSIGNMENTS** and understand their reflective nature regarding my professional development.
- [ ] I understand that the course is **FEE-BASED** and that the CC@E must **MANUALLY REGISTER** me for the course during the quarter when I do my internship.
- [ ] I will regularly check the **CANVAS COURSE** for announcements, as well as to submit assignments by their due dates.
- [ ] I understand that I must receive a **70% on EACH assignment** to receive **CREDIT**.

> **Note for international students:** You MUST receive credit for this course in order to protect OPT eligibility.

---

## Section 3 — Previous Credit Check

**Have you previously received ENGR 321/601 credit for this IDENTICAL INTERNSHIP?**
*(Same employer, role, pay rate, and hours per week from a previous quarter)*

- [ ] **No** — this is an internship I haven't received credit for *(most common)*
- [ ] **Yes** — I'm continuing a role with the EXACT SAME DETAILS (including hours/week) from a previous quarter

---

## Section 4 — Visa Status

**Are you on an ACTIVE F-1 STUDENT VISA?**

- [ ] **Yes** — I'm an F-1 student
- [ ] **No** — I'm a domestic student

> **Note:** If you are an international student, you will be asked to provide additional information to satisfy visa requirements and verify CPT/OPT eligibility.  
> If you are a **J-1 student**, consult with your academic advising team & ISS before proceeding.

---

## Section 5 — Employer / Internship Information

### Employer Name
- **Field:** Company or organization name
- **Required:** Yes

### Employer Physical Address
- **Field:** Street address, city, state, zip code (include country if outside the US)
- **Required:** Yes

### Internship Start Date
- **Field:** Date (MM/DD/YYYY)
- **Required:** Yes

### Internship End Date
- **Field:** Date (MM/DD/YYYY)
- **Required:** Yes

### Pay Rate
- **Field:** Amount with timeframe (e.g., `$15/hour`, `$4,000/month`, or `Unpaid`)
- **Required:** Yes

### Number of Work Hours Per Week
- **Required:** Yes
- **Note:** This determines the number of credits you will be registered for.

| Hours per Week | Credit Type |
|---|---|
| 1–20 hours | Part-time (1 credit) |
| 21+ hours | Full-time (2 credits) |

### Work Location Type
- **Required:** Yes
- [ ] **In-person** (at the employer's address listed above)
- [ ] **Virtual**
- [ ] **Hybrid**

> If **Hybrid**: specify how many hours per week you will physically work at the employer's address.

### Internship Duties Description
- **Field:** Describe your internship duties, including specific examples of general responsibilities and/or intern projects.
- **Required:** Yes
- **Note:** The description is used to determine if your role is **at least 50% engineering** to qualify for academic credit. Roles that do not meet this requirement will **not** be approved.

---

## Section 6 — Offer Letter Upload

**Upload a copy of your Offer Letter.**

**File naming convention:** `<LastName>_<StudentID#>`
*(Example: `Cornelius_1234567`)*

### The offer letter must include:

1. Description of your roles & responsibilities
2. Start and end dates of your internship
3. Number of hours you will be working per week
4. Company or organizational letterhead

### Additional requirements for continuing internships:

If you are continuing your internship from a previous quarter or working full-time, also include a **recently dated (within the last month) verification of employment letter** containing the above information.

**Accepted alternatives if the letter is not recently dated:**
- A recently-dated paystub
- An email from your HR representative or employer verifying current employment

> **Important:** If your document is not recently dated, it will most likely **not be accepted**.  
> If you are applying early for CPT authorization, you still need the required documentation. **Do not fill out this form until you have all required documents.**

**Upload limits:** Up to 5 files, max 1 GB per file.
