The intern will undergo structured onboarding training covering essential engineering tools and workflows, including but not limited to:

- Version control and collaborative development using **Git** and **GitHub**
- Code review workflows and pull request management
- Internal toolchain orientation and documentation standards
- Engineering best practices and project management methodologies

**Phase 2 — AI Teaching System Development (Weeks 6–15)**

The intern will be integrated into the company's core engineering team and will actively contribute to the development and optimization of EasyScaleCloud's AI-powered teaching system. Specific responsibilities include:

- **Improvement & Evaluation:** Analyzing system performance and identifying areas for enhancement

The ultimate goal of this internship is to contribute meaningfully to the optimization of EasyScaleCloud's AI teaching system workflow, delivering measurable improvements to the product.
